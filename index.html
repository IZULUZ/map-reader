❓ HOW TO PLAY

1. Explorer

Deduce your destination from the 'Situation'. You must ask the Guide for directions first via Walkie-Talkie. When your movement ends, click [Move Complete] to ask for more. Once you receive the arrival signal, press [Location Check].

2. Guide

You can see the map but do not know the destination. Give directions based on the Explorer's questions. When you see the Explorer reach the target, click [📢 Signal Arrival].

👮 Checkpoint Missions

At the center checkpoint, the password appears only on the Guide's screen. Odd rounds: Fill-in-the-blank typing. Even rounds: Directional Combo (Buttons). Communicate via Walkie-Talkie!
